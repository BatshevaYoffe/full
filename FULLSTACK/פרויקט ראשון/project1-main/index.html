ABOUT

The B&BS set in Eilat and with short distance from the Beaches Guest House-heated swimming pool offers express check-in and check-out, non-smoking rooms, an outdoor swimming pool, free WiFi throughout the property and a garden. Boasting family rooms, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. The accommodation provides a shared kitchen, and luggage storage for guests. All rooms are fitted with a private bathroom fitted with a shower and free toiletries, while some rooms also offer a kitchenette equipped with a microwave. At the guest house, all rooms are equipped with air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. The property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
